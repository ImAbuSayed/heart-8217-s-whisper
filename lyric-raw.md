<p>Heart&#8217;s Whisper</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-lyrics">Lyrics</h2>



<p>[Verse 1]<br>In the shadows of my heart tonight,<br>Where memories linger and dreams take flight.<br>Your voice echoes, soft and sweet,<br>Once together, now incomplete.</p>



<p>[Chorus]<br>Oh, the heart keeps whispering your name,<br>Every beat, every tear, it&#8217;s the same.<br>Can&#8217;t hide the longing, though we&#8217;re apart,<br>Feel the rhythm of a broken heart.</p>



<p>[Verse 2]<br>Stars in the sky share my silent cry,<br>Wish upon them, hope never dies.<br>In the breeze, I hear your song,<br>Feeling you close, where you belong.</p>



<p>[Bridge]<br>Through the night, I&#8217;ll dream of you,<br>Under the moon, love feels so true.<br>In every breath, I sense your grace,<br>Stay forever in this secret place.</p>



<p>[Outro]<br>Heart&#8217;s whisper, you&#8217;re my guiding light,<br>Through the pain, you&#8217;ll see me fight.<br>With each dawn, I&#8217;ll start anew,<br>But heart&#8217;s whisper will always hold you.</p>
